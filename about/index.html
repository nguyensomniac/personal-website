About + Contact

I am currently looking for opportunities for Spring/Summer 2015. I would prefer a UI/UX internship, but I will consider software engineering positions.

I’m currently studying computer science at UC Berkeley, but what keeps me up at night is thinking about better ways to design the world around me. When given a choice between a perfect grid and an efficient algorithm, I’ll most likely choose the former.

I learn most by doing - for me, a weekend at a hackathon is more rewarding than a semester in a classroom. I also help organize events for Hackers@Berkeley and Tequity to encourage others to find a new love for making things, especially those in underrepresented groups or those who haven’t had the resources to learn earlier on.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling and live music. I also try to write when I can - previously, I wrote about startups for berkeleyByte. Regrettably, I spend a disproportionate amount of my disposable income on boba.
