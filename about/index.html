I'm Lily, a designer, developer, and hacker at UC Berkeley.

I spend much of my time thinking about how to display information in an easily digestable and meaningful manner. From creating data visualizations, rapidly prototyping, or wrangling with the pen tool, I will use whatever resources I have at my disposal to do so.

I like to play around with all things JavaScript (mostly on the front-end, but sometimes on the back-end), but I've also worked on projects in Python and Ruby. Whenever I go to hackathons, I try to learn a new framework or otherwise step outside my comfort zone.

Besides the above, I write about tech and startups for BerkeleyByte and hold an officer position in Hackers@Berkeley. I am also constantly in pursuit of good music and new places to explore.

Want to hire me, share your favorite albums, or otherwise just want to talk? E-mail me at lcn@berkeley.edu.
