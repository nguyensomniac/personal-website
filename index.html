About

I am an interdisciplinary creative based in Berkeley, CA. I currently study computer science at UC Berkeley and am addicted to pushing pixels, crunching code, and delving into data.

I currently lead design for Tequity and create interactives for The Daily Californian. Before that, I created products at Scalable Press, planned events at Hackers@Berkeley, and wrote about startups for berkeleyByte.

When I'm not hacking, you might catch me exploring the Bay or watching a concert. Regrettably, I spend a disproportionate amount of my income on bubble tea.
